Be a Psychic Predictor!

Can you predict which letter from the alphabet the computer will randomly choose?

Directions:

  • Choose your prediction by pressing a letter between a - z.
  • You have 5 chances to predict the computer's selection
  • If you successfully predict the computer's selection, you win!
  • Otherwise you'll lose a chance to predict, if run out of chances you lose, don't be a loser
